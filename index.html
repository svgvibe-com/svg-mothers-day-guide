Why Use SVG Files for Your Cricut Projects?

SVG (Scalable Vector Graphics) files are a must-have for digital crafting. They maintain crisp lines at any size, and they’re compatible with Cricut Design Space and Silhouette Studio. With SVGs, you can:

  • Create intricate vinyl decals for mugs or glasses
  • Design heat-transfer shirts or tote bags
  • Make layered paper shadowboxes or wall art
  • Print custom stickers, tags, or greeting cards
